About us

Alpha Psi Lambda Crest

Alpha Psi Lambda is the nation's First and Largest Co-ed Latino oriented Social Greek Fraternity. We are family-based organization that breaks through sterotypical boundaries and eases its way into a plethora of cultures. We are a unique Greek Fraternity where only the Noblest Brothers and Sisters come together to form the great family that Alpha Psi Lambda represents.
